Who do we care for?

We provide comprehensive care for persons dealing with:

  • Depression
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Anxiety disorders
  • Schizophrenia
  • Personality disorders
  • Substance abuse and diverse addictions
  • Others

Our treatment approach is inclusive of pharmacotherapy and psychotherapy interventions depending on the case presentation.

We provide both 24/7 in-patient care at our 40 bed facility and out-patient care at our clinic. Telepsychiatry consultations are also available.
