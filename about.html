About African Institute of Mental Health

Glad to know you'd love to learn more about us.

At Institute of Mental Health (AIMH), we appreciate the fact that a healthy family is a happy family.

In view of this, we pride ourselves on providing quality and excellent patient satisfaction. Happy Family Hospital is located right in the heart of the city of Lagos, Nigeria. It is a unique specialist hospital established to cater to the general medical and psychiatric needs of people.

The Hospital has a full complement of well-trained licensed and experienced general and psychiatric doctors, nurses, and other personnel to ensure smooth day-to-day operations of the hospital as well as the satisfaction of our customers.

Our vision

The vision of Institute of Mental Health (AIMH) is to serve Nigeria by providing quality care in a serene, non-stigmatized environment for the treatment of patients (general and psychiatric). It also provides support for medical and scientific research.

Our promise and values

We will discharge our responsibilities to our patients and society to the best of our ability. Our success lies in excellent patient care which is continuously refined through interactions with our stakeholders. We trust our highly motivated and professional staff who are steeped in AIMH’s unique patient care practices to deliver value beyond the patients’ or caregivers’ expectations. AIMH shall remain a centre of excellence in psychiatric services while promoting advocacy for mental health as well as adopting the latest practices and technology in mental health treatment.
